The Shop

Our Facility

230,000 square feet built for production at scale.

DSW's facility sits in Birmingham's industrial corridor — the same district that helped build American infrastructure for over a century. Since 2004, we've expanded that footprint into one of the most capable fabrication floors in the region.

Six fiber lasers. Three tube laser machines. Five Mitsubishi press brakes and a robotic bending cell. Four OTC Daihen weld robots alongside manual weld stations. Automation towers that load, run, and unload sheet stock through the night. All of it supported by a FARO arm CMM and laser scanner for first-article and in-process inspection.

We run two shifts, nearly 24 hours a day — because our customers' programs don't slow down, and neither do we.

Operations

How We Run the Floor

Every part that leaves DSW runs through the same disciplined process — from raw material receipt to final inspection. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

01 — Material

Material In

Sheet, plate, tube, and structural stock received, logged, and staged by job. Carbon steel, stainless, and aluminum managed separately.

02 — Cutting

Laser & Plasma

Fiber laser and high-definition plasma cells run nested programs from engineering. Automation towers handle unmanned sheet cycling for high-volume jobs.

03 — Forming

Press Brake & Roll

Five Mitsubishi press brakes — including a robot-assisted cell for repeat bends — plus a Davi plate roller for curved and rolled profiles.

04 — Welding

Robotic & Manual

Four OTC Daihen weld robots handle high-volume assemblies. Manual weld stations cover complex fixtures and lower-volume structural work.

05 — Secondary

Finish & Hardware

Spot welding, rivet installation, and tapping arm (to 16mm) for hardware insertion. Parts leave ready for assembly or finishing.

06 — Inspection

CMM & Dimensional

FARO arm CMM and laser scanner on-site. First-article inspection documented and provided on request. PPAP capability available.

07 — Jig & Fixture

In-House Tooling

DSW designs and builds its own jigs and fixtures internally. Faster setup, tighter repeatability, no outsourcing wait.

08 — Ship

Packaged & Out

Parts staged, labeled, and shipped to customer spec. Partner vendor services available for powder coat, plating, and assembly integration.
