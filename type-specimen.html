About DSW

Birmingham Steel, Since 2004.

DSW Cutting started in a knife shop in Birmingham's industrial district. Today it's 230,000 square feet of fiber laser, tube laser, robotic forming, and precision welding — all under one roof.

Under the leadership of CEO Chris McIlvaine, DSW has built its reputation on a simple principle: do the work right, stand behind it, and treat customers like partners. That philosophy — paired with a serious investment in modern fabrication technology — is why manufacturers across trucking, agriculture, power infrastructure, and industrial equipment keep coming back.

We run two shifts, nearly 24 hours a day. Our FARO arm CMM and laser scanner mean first-article approval is a process, not a prayer. And when your program scales, we scale with it — without making you start over with a new vendor.
