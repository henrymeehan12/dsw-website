How It Works

From First Contact to Final Part.

01

Get a Quote

Send your drawings. Our sales engineers — people with real manufacturing backgrounds — review every quote before it goes out. Accurate pricing, fast turnaround.

02

First-Piece Verification

Every new part runs through first-piece inspection before full production starts. FARO arm CMM confirms dimensions. No surprises on the production run.

03

Produce & Deliver

Laser, bend, weld, and finish — all under one roof, one point of contact. Parts staged and shipped to your spec. We own the outcome from order to delivery.
