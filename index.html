Solutions for All Your Needs

DSW is at the forefront of fabrication technology in the Southeast. From light gauge sheet to heavy structural profiles — laser cutting, bending, welding, and finishing under one roof.

Cutting

Laser Cutting & Plasma

2× Mitsubishi 6kW 2× Mitsubishi 8kW 2× Mitsubishi 10kW AI-Assisted Cutting Conditions 2 Material Towers Lights-Out Production Up to 1" Plate 2× Messer Plasma Plasma to 2" Carbon · Stainless · Aluminum
Six Mitsubishi Fiber Laser systems — 6kW, 8kW, and 10kW — with AI-assisted cutting condition optimization that adapts in real time for consistent edge quality across every material and thickness. Two automated material towers enable lights-out production: autonomous material handling and overnight runs without operators on the floor. Two Messer plasma cutters handle Carbon plate to 2" thick with bevel capability. Light gauge sheet to 1" plate in Carbon, Stainless, and Aluminum.

Cutting

Tube Laser Cutting

BLM LT8 Hytube Titan Up to 40 ft In/Out Up to 12.7" Diameter 2D & 3D Bevel Round · Square · Structural C-Channel · Angle · I-Beam AR-Tube Design Software
Two dedicated tube laser systems — the BLM LT8 and the Hytube Titan — among the most capable tube laser platforms in the Southeast. 2D and 3D bevel cutting of round tubes, square tubes, structural beams, C-channels, angles, and I-beams. Up to 40 ft in/out. Complex intersecting joints and assembly prep come off the machine ready to weld — no secondary operations needed.

Press Brake Bending

250-Ton Press Brake FANUC R-2000iC Fully Autonomous Bending Cell Up to 330 lbs Up to 14 ft Long Lights-Out Capable
A 250-ton press brake paired with a FANUC R-2000iC robotic arm delivers a fully autonomous bending cell — loading, forming, and offloading parts without an operator.
