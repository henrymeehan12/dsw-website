Solutions for All Your Needs

DSW is at the forefront of fabrication technology in the Southeast. From light gauge sheet to heavy structural profiles — laser cutting, bending, welding, and finishing under one roof.

Cutting

Laser Cutting & Plasma

Mitsubishi GX Fiber Laser AI-Assisted Cutting Conditions Tower-Fed Automation Up to 1" Plate Plasma to 2" Carbon · Stainless · Aluminum Bevel Capable
DSW operates Mitsubishi GX Fiber Laser systems with AI-assisted cutting condition optimization — the machine learns and adjusts parameters in real time for consistent edge quality across material types and thicknesses. Tower-fed automation delivers maximum throughput. From light gauge sheet to 1" plate in Carbon, Stainless, and Aluminum. High-definition Plasma cutting handles Carbon plate to 2" thick with bevel capability.
